Class like/hate table

This table will show you whether a person or monster class will like, hate, ignore, or fear a different class of person or monster.

It works as left » top, for example alien prey fears alien predator, and human military hates alien military.

Bare in mind that anything that shows up as allied to players (green monster info text) will always be classed as player ally rather than its default class.
